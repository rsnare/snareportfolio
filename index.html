About

I'm a product operations leader who builds the systems, rhythms, and tools that help product and engineering organizations ship better work, faster. My career has spanned fintech, ecommerce, and SaaS - from co-founding a company in Peru to leading product operations teams at Shopify, Stripe, and Klarna.

Today, I'm the Director of Product Strategy & Operations at ActiveCampaign, where I lead the function for a 250-person EPD organization. I'm particularly focused on how AI is reshaping the way product ops teams operate - from how we synthesize customer feedback to how we track and communicate roadmap progress.

Before all of this, I started my career at Google and earned my MBA at Kellogg. I'm a native English speaker, professionally proficient in Spanish, and spent nearly a decade living in Lima, Peru.
